Global Warming

Climate change is affecting flora and fauna across the Artic, the home of polar bears. Polar bears are having trouble finding food with all the sea ice they depend on melting!

Ocean Polution

This pregnant sperm whale washed up dead in Italy with 50lbs of plastic in its stomach.

Extreme Flooding

Extreme weather such as floods are occuring all over the globe as an effect of climate change. This photo was taken in Venice in St. Mark's Square.

Wild Fires

Climate change is causing extreme weather patterns including stronger winds that make wild fires such as this one in Southern California worse than they traditionally are.

Poaching

Elephant tusks are a hot commodity on the black market. Law enforcement destroys the ivory so it doesn't return to the black market.

Dog Consumption

Look at these cute dogs. Consuming dog meat is a common practice in parts of Asia. The tradition is on the decline as younger generations consider dogs to be pets versus food.

Deforestation

Half of the orangutan population has been lost due to man-induced deforestation to make room for livestock raising and palm oil plantations. Deforestation is a leading cause of climate change.

Climate Change

Climate change is causing extreme weather patterns across the globe leading to severe droughts in many parts of the world. This family in Niger uses 60 liters of water daily compared to 1000 liters of water used daily by a family in New York City.

Ocean Pollution

A whale shark swims beside a plastic bag in the Gulf of Aden near Yemen. Whale sharks are the biggest fish in the sea, but are still threatened by ingesting small bits of plastic.

"Medicinal" Trade

Pangolins are the most trafficked animal in the world. Its scales and meat are heavily traded. In 2016, 7.3 tonnes of pangolin scales were seized in Hong Kong, with a street value worth 2 million USD.

Poaching

Rhino poaching is a practice that occurs because rhino horns are sold lucratively on the black market for "medicinal use". Certain rhino populations are on the brink of extinction due to this illicit practice. This young white rhino resting after being transferred to a safer habitat in Botswana.

Fur Trade

Not only are rabbits raised and slaughtered to serve as meat, older rabbits are raised and killed for their fur, which is used in clothing, flyfishing lures, and trim on craft items.

War

There are a number of everlasting conflicts happening throughout the Middle East, as well as certain parts of Africa and Asia. There are few wars that have been more devasted than Syria. Of the at least 101 journalists killed in Syria since 2011, 27% were photographers.

Ocean Plastic

Plastic wrapping like this can permanently harm young marine animals, choking or disfiguring them.

Factory Farming

The conditions in which livestock is raised in is usually gross and inhumane. We need to take a hard look at our food system and ponder the principles of profit vs health vs morality.

Fur Trade

85% of the fur industry's skins come from animals on fur factory farms, in which the conditions are filthy and unsanitary to say the least.
